Sum of Even Numbers
While Loop

Objective: Loop through a range of numbers and accumulate the sum of the even numbers.

Use a while loop for the same result as Exercise 1, but summing only the even values.

Requirements

  • Name your file sum_of_evens.cpp
  • Prompt the user to enter a positive integer n.
  • Initialize a counter variable before the loop.
  • Use a while loop to iterate from 1 to n.
  • Inside the loop, check whether the current number is even using modulo.
  • If it is even, add it to a running total.
  • After the loop completes, print the final sum of even numbers from 1 to n.

Sample Output

Enter a positive integer: 10
The sum of even numbers from 1 to 10 is 30.
