Lab 04 — Submission Instructions

This lab consists of five C++ exercises. Each exercise must produce one .cpp file, using the filenames listed below.

Follow the steps on this page to prepare and submit your Lab04.zip file.

Files & Filenames

Your Lab04 folder must contain at least these files:

    Lab04
    ├── sum_of_odds.cpp
    ├── sum_of_evens.cpp
    ├── number_guessing.cpp
    ├── password_retry.cpp
    └── multiplication_table.cpp
  • No subfolders inside Lab04 — all files should be at the top level.
  • Compiled executables may be present. They will be ignored and will not affect your grade.
  • Extra files (project files, temp files, etc.) will also be ignored.
Reminder!

Required File Header in Each .cpp

All your .cpp files must begin with the standard lab header.

Missing or incomplete headers may result in point deductions.

Creating Lab04.zip
Windows
  1. Right-click the Lab04 folder.
  2. Select Send to → Compressed (zipped) folder.
  3. Rename the resulting file to Lab04.zip.
macOS
  1. Right-click (or two-finger click) the Lab04 folder.
  2. Select Compress "Lab04".
  3. This creates Lab04.zip in the same directory.
