Sum of Odd Numbers
For Loop

Objective: Loop through a range of numbers and accumulate the sum of the odd numbers.

Requirements

  • Name your file sum_of_odds.cpp
  • Prompt the user to enter a positive integer n.
  • Use a for loop to iterate from 1 to n.
  • Inside the loop, check if the current number is odd using modulo.
  • If it is odd, add it to a running total.
  • After the loop, print the final sum of odd numbers from 1 to n.

Sample Output

Enter a positive integer: 10
The sum of odd numbers from 1 to 10 is 25.
