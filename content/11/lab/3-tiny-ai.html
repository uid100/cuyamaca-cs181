Assignment: Build a Tiny AI Text Generator (With Starter Code)

In this assignment, you will build a simple AI-like program that generates text by learning patterns from a sentence. This mimics, at a very small scale, how real AI language models work.

Your task is to complete a partially written program that:

  • Reads a sentence
  • Breaks it into words
  • Stores word relationships
  • Generates new text using those relationships

The structure is already provided — your job is to complete the TODO sections.

Key Idea: Predicting the Next Word

If your program sees: 

the cat sits on the mat

It will learn:

  • "the" → cat, mat
  • "cat" → sits

Then it can generate new text like: 

the cat sits on the cat sits
